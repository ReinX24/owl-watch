About Owl Watch_

Mission

Owl Watch aims to provide consultation and resources regarding the mental health of students and people in general.

Vision

Owl Watch envisions a future where technology is fully utilized to address the mental health concerns of people.

Objectives

  • Provide psychological counseling for students and teachers.
  • Make use of technology to to address mental health concerns.
  • Provide contacts to mental health professionals and resources.
